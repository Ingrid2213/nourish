Welcome to the Feed The Artists Online Reservation System.

This system is designed to be used by three groups of people:

  • Regional Event Organizers
  • Theme Camp Organizers
  • Artist Group Organizers

If you are a participant in an enrolled theme camp or artist group, please contact your group organizer for more information about Feed The Artists.

If you are organizing a theme camp or artists group that is attended an event in our system, please find your event on the events page. From there, you can register your group and start coordinating meal sharing!

If you are an event organizer, you can register your event.
